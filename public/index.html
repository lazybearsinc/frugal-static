Common Questions

What exactly does this service do?

We monitor prices at Costco locations throughout the United States and Canada, and we alert you when there’s a drop in the cost of your purchases. Just share your receipt with us, and we’ll keep an eye out for savings opportunities.

How do I get started?

Simply scan or click the QR code above to connect via WhatsApp. Then send a photo of your receipt - we'll handle the price tracking automatically for the next 30 days.

Is this affiliated with Costco?

No - we're an independent price tracking tool created by a Costco member. You'll need to request any price adjustments directly from Costco following their standard process.

How is my receipt data protected?

We only extract purchase dates, items, and prices. Your data is encrypted, never shared, and automatically deleted after the 30-day tracking period.

Is this service really free?

Yes! This is a free tool with no fees or commissions. It's a passion project created to help fellow Costco members never miss savings opportunities.
